How It Works

The System Behind the Signals.

01
Daily Market Scan
Each morning, the system scans ~24 Wheel-suitable tickers for IV Rank, liquidity, and technical conditions. Only setups that pass all filters surface.
02
Strike Evaluation
Live Greeks from ORATS are used to evaluate candidate strikes against your configured delta range and DTE window. MACD phase and support/resistance checks run as additional filters you can enable or disable.
03
Regime Filter
VIX level determines parameter tightness. Elevated volatility means tighter strikes, smaller size, or no trade. The system respects market conditions.
04
Order Execution & Management
Limit orders are placed at optimal prices and walked if needed for fills. Positions are monitored intraday — delta breaches, earnings windows, and expiry triggers auto-roll or close without hesitation.
05
AI Journal & Review
Every trade is logged with entry rationale and Greeks against your configured rules. A morning briefing and EOD summary show how your positions performed relative to your own parameters — educational context for your own analysis.
⚡
Unlike weekly put-selling bots, WheelBot targets 21–45 DTE with live IV Rank filtering — more premium, less whipsaw. VIX-gated, ORATS-sourced, delta-targeted — not just sell-the-closest-expiry.
